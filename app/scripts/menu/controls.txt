Note of all controls:

Left controller:
    Grip: show item placement menu
    Trigger: rotate current item preview
    Joystick up/down: swap item groups
    Joystick left/right: swap current item
    Bottom button: undo placement
    Top button: place current item

Right controller:
    Joystick: teleport
    Bottom button: open and close menu (ending audio)
    Grip: close menu (not ending audio)
